My name is Martin and I'm a recent Mathematics graduate from the University of Waterloo. The best part about Waterloo is it let me try several careers before graduating: accounting, consulting, and finance. Luckily, I fell in love with tech!

Working at Facebook, along with joining the prestigious Next 36 program, took me down a whirlwind path of entrepreneurship and technology. Over the past year, I've worked with a team of amazing Waterloo grads on two startups: Versionly and Practicure.

Versionly is version control for PowerPoint! We created this to solve a problem I faced everyday in several jobs: not knowing what changed in the latest PowerPoint presentation. Anyone can simply cc: team@versionly.com when sharing a PPT over email, and Versionly automatically tracks the changes!




PractiCure is a device that helps dentists increase the lifespan of their cavity fillings by up to 10 years! My co-founder had several years of experience in dental where he identified this problem. We even garnered enough attention to receive a cease and desist from a competitor,just 4 weeks in!

Speaking of adventures, I love sports: mountain biking, running, tennis, and even dodgeball. You name it, I play it.

I also volunteer and help out others any chance I get (including up-and-coming entrepreneurs). Reach out to me anytime.
